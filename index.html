About Us

Complete Healthcare at
Banibihar Main Road, Aska

Debabhumi Medical Store & General Diagnostic Lab is your neighbourhood healthcare destination on Banibihar Main Road, Aska. We combine a fully-stocked medicine store with a state-of-the-art diagnostic laboratory to provide holistic healthcare services to every family.

Our lab is equipped with modern automated analysers ensuring fast & accurate reports. From routine blood tests to specialised hormone panels, all types of blood tests are available at affordable prices. Certified technicians handle every sample with precision, and reports are delivered same-day for most tests.
